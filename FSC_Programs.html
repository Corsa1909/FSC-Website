The Journey

What the Process Actually Looks Like

From your first conversation to the day you leave Mexico with your baby — an honest overview of each stage.

1

Free Consultation

We walk through your situation together — your medical history, family configuration, budget range, and timeline. By the end, you'll know which program makes sense for your case and what to expect at every stage. No commitment required.

2

Program Start & Matching

Once you're enrolled, we begin surrogate matching from our pre-screened pool. For IVF programs, you'll also select an egg donor from our database. We present profiles and you make the decision — we don't rush this step.

3

IVF & Embryo Transfer

Our medical team manages stimulation, retrieval, fertilization, and embryo transfer at our partner clinic in Cancún. You'll receive updates at each milestone. For FET programs, this step begins with transport coordination.

4

Pregnancy & Legal in Parallel

Once pregnancy is confirmed, full monitoring begins. At the same time, attorney Buenrostro initiates the Amparo pre-process. You receive regular updates throughout — you're never waiting without knowing why.

5

Birth, Amparo & Registration

Your baby is born in a private hospital. The Amparo is granted and the birth certificate is issued in your name(s) only — typically within 2 weeks of birth. We guide you through the next steps for your home country's consulate process.
